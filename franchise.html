Partner with us!

We are constantly looking forward to collaborate with people all around the world who share the same ideology of bringing “happiness outside the shell”. We always keep our franchise requirements fair and easy to understand. This makes it easier for you to start doing business with us in no time. Let’s just say our franchising process is as fast as a sports car provided you have the minimal requirements all set.

Following are our minimal franchising requirements:-

  • Restaurant property must have a minimum 1000 square feet carpet area.

  • Restaurant must have 24*7 electricity. (Power backup is must if electricity supply not constant)

  • Restaurant must have constant water supply and necessary plumbing.

  • Restaurant must have proper garbage disposal facilities.

  • Property must be located in a clean and upscale place. Surrounded with a good number of potential customers.

  • Restaurant owners must adhere to our customer relationship and hospitality guidelines.

  • Restaurant must have a computerised POS system.

  • Restaurant must adhere with all the legal compliance laws for opening a restaurant business which are stated by the Government of India as on date.

  • Restaurant must have an initial investment of 5,00,000 INR.

  • Note: We are always open to provide assistance in helping you meet our minimal franchising requirements. Just click on “Franchisee Enquiry” to fill the form so that we can reach you.
