We are constantly looking forward to collaborate with people all around the world who share the same ideology of bringing “happiness out of the shell”. We always keep our franchise requirements fair and easy to understand. This makes it easier for you to start doing business with us in no time. Let’s just say our franchising process is as fast as a sports car provided you have the minimal requirements all set.

Following are some of our minimal franchising requirements:-

Restaurant property must have a minimum carpet area of 500 to 700 square feet.
Restaurant must have a 24*7 electricity supply (Power backup mandatory if electricity supply is not constant).
Restaurant must have a constant water supply with necessary plumbing.
Restaurant must have proper garbage disposal facilities.
Restaurant owners must adhere to our customer relationship guidelines.
Property must be located in a clean and upscale place. Surrounded with a good number of potential customers.
Ground floor location is preferred for better customer penetration.
Restaurant must adhere with all the legal compliance laws for opening a restaurant business which are stated by the Government of India as on date.
